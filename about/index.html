About

Hi there! I'm Christina — a masterful writer, editor, and communicator who values precision, accuracy, nuance, and style. My expertise spans across print, digital, and social media formats.

I am a passion-driven individual with a never-stop-learning attitude who thrives in collaborative spaces. Native to the newsroom, I am an efficient worker and natural leader in high-pressure, fast-paced environments.

I earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in journalism from Lehigh University, where I graduated magna cum laude in 2023. While at Lehigh, I served as a five-time editor for The Brown and White, where I ended my tenure as Editor-in-Chief. In my young career, I have written and/or produced content for more than 10 publications and organizations.

The hats I don are not confined to professional fashion. I am a music enthusiast, highly skilled in playlist curation but also devoted to the art of the album; a lover of literature, adept in setting ambitious reading goals; an experimental cook and mixologist, proficient in measuring with the heart (when appropriate); and a coffee connoisseur with a predilection for the French press and small cafés. I am a New York native, ambitious dreamer, and believer in kindness and compassion.
